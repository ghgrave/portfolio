Your New Home


Imperial Gardens Apartments in Lockhart, TX has opened it's Section 8 wait list for applicants. Operating under a grant from HUD, approved tenants pay a percent of their monthly income as rent. We offer wait list preferences to applicants that are employed, retired or receiving disability income from Social Security.

Our small apartment community features 2 and 3 bedroom floor plans with central air and heat. Kitchens are equipped with an electric range and refrigerator/freezer. We also have an on-site laundry facility. Applications may be obtained at our Management Office located at the Springfield Villas Apartments, 1300 Pancho Street in Lockhart. Interested persons should contact us to set an appointment time to make application.
