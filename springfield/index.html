Welcome Home


Springfield Villas is a 32 Unit Apartment Community in Lockhart, TX. We feature large common areas, ample parking, private entrances, and large apartments. Our Community is designated as affordable, and tenants must not exceed certain income restrictions.

Our property is locally owned and operated by Steven Holman & Chris Jackson. We have owned and operated apartment communities in Lockhart for over 11 years. Springfield Villas is normally full and always accepts applications to fill future vacancies.

The MAXIMUM allowable income is listed below:


  • 1 Person Annual Income MUST be below $33,660
  • 2 Person Annual Income MUST be below $38,460
  • 3 Person Annual Income MUST be below $43,260
  • 4 Person Annual Income MUST be below $48,060
  • 5 Person Annual Income MUST be below $51,960
